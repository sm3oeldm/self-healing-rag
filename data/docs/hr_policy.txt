NovaTech Inc. - HR Policy Document

Vacation & Time Off:
Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid vacation per year.
Vacation days reset on January 1st and do not roll over to the next year.
Employees must request vacation at least 2 weeks in advance using the HR portal.

Remote Work:
NovaTech operates on a hybrid model. Employees are expected in the office 3 days per week.
Remote work on Mondays and Fridays is permitted for all full-time staff.
Contractors and part-time staff are fully remote.

Health Benefits:
All full-time employees receive health, dental, and vision insurance starting on day 1.
NovaTech covers 90% of the monthly premium for the employee and 60% for dependents.

Performance Reviews:
Performance reviews are conducted twice a year, in June and December.
Salary adjustments are made following the December review cycle.
