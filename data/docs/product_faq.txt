NovaTech DataPilot - Product FAQ

What is DataPilot?
DataPilot is NovaTech's flagship product. It is an AI-powered analytics dashboard
that connects to a company's existing data sources and generates automated insights,
reports, and forecasts without requiring any coding knowledge.

What data sources does DataPilot support?
DataPilot integrates with Google Sheets, Microsoft Excel, Salesforce, HubSpot,
PostgreSQL, MySQL, and most REST APIs.

How much does DataPilot cost?
DataPilot is priced at $299 per month for up to 5 users.
Enterprise plans with unlimited users start at $999 per month.
A 14-day free trial is available with no credit card required.

What kind of support does NovaTech offer?
All DataPilot plans include email support with a 24-hour response time.
Enterprise plans include a dedicated account manager and phone support.

Is DataPilot secure?
DataPilot is SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest.
NovaTech does not sell or share customer data with third parties.
