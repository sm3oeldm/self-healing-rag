NovaTech Inc. - Company Overview

NovaTech Inc. was founded in 2018 and is headquartered in Austin, Texas.
The company specializes in AI-powered data analytics software for small and medium businesses.
NovaTech currently employs 142 people across engineering, sales, and operations.
The CEO of NovaTech is Sarah Chen, who co-founded the company with CTO Marcus Obi.
NovaTech's annual revenue as of 2023 is $18 million.
The company has offices in Austin, New York, and London.
NovaTech's core mission is to make enterprise-grade AI tools accessible to businesses of all sizes.
